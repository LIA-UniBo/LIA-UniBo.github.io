AIII Projects 2021/2022

This page contains a list of potential topics for theses, internships, and course projects from the LIA/AI group at University of Bologna.

  • Besides picking a topic from the list, students can proposed their own topics. If you have an industrial problem you are familiar with, a research idea that you would like to pursue, or even just a hobby or personal interest, you can propose it as a topic for your activity.
  • In any case, before startin your activity you should contact the course teachers to agree on the details
  • Additional information on each topic can be obtained by contacting the course teachers. Feel free to ask about a few themes that you find interesting
