AITI Projects 2021/2022

This page contains a list of potential topics for theses, internships, and course projects from the LIA/AI group at University of Bologna.

  1. Besides picking a topic from the list, students can propose their own topics. If you have an industrial problem you are familiar with, a research idea that you would like to pursue, or even just a hobby or personal interest, you can propose it as a topic for your activity.
  2. In any case, before starting your activity you should contact the course teachers to agree on the details
  3. Additional information on each topic can be obtained by contacting the course teachers. Feel free to ask about a few themes that you find interesting
